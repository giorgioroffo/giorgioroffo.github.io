I am a PhD researcher and Head of AI with over 13 years of experience across academia and industry. My research spans computer vision, multimodal AI, medical imaging, attention and feature selection, and LLM systems. I have translated fundamental research into deployed products and high-impact publications at venues including TPAMI, ICCV, MICCAI, CHI, and ACM Multimedia.

My core methodological contribution is Infinite Feature Selection (Inf-FS), a graph-theoretic framework for feature ranking published in IEEE TPAMI (2020) and ICCV (2015, 2017), now extended to Infinite Self-Attention (InfSA) — a spectral reformulation enabling linear-cost multi-hop attention in Transformers. I have led AI teams delivering FDA-cleared medical AI systems, research for autonomous driving, and production-grade agentic AI platforms.

Currently leading AI initiatives in agentic systems and LLM alignment at Equixly. Open to selected research collaborations, invited talks, and advisory work.
